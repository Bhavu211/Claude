Senior Product Manager — Risk & Fraud
Razorpay | Bengaluru, India (Hybrid) | Full-time

About the team
Razorpay's Risk & Fraud team builds the systems that keep our payment
platform safe for millions of merchants while keeping friction near zero for
legitimate transactions. We sit at the intersection of product, data science,
and compliance, and we move fast — priorities shift weekly as new fraud
patterns emerge, so you'll need to be comfortable making calls with
incomplete information.

What you'll do
- Own the end-to-end roadmap for merchant risk scoring and fraud detection
  products
- Partner with data science and engineering to ship and iterate on ML-based
  risk models
- Define and track the KPIs that matter: fraud loss rate, false positive
  rate, merchant onboarding time, and dispute resolution TAT
- Work directly with Compliance and Legal to ensure product decisions hold up
  under RBI and PCI-DSS scrutiny
- Write clear PRDs, run sprint planning with a pod of 5-6 engineers, and
  represent the risk roadmap in quarterly business reviews
- Mentor 1-2 junior PMs on the broader Payments product team

Requirements
- 5+ years of product management experience, with at least 2 years owning a
  fraud, risk, trust & safety, or compliance-adjacent product
- Must have hands-on experience shipping products backed by ML/scoring models
  (you don't need to build the model, but you need to know how to work with
  one)
- Strong SQL skills — you will pull your own data for prioritization
  decisions
- Demonstrated experience translating regulatory requirements (RBI, PCI-DSS,
  or equivalent) into product requirements
- Experience managing or mentoring other PMs
- Excellent written communication — you will write PRDs and present to
  leadership regularly

Preferred qualifications
- Prior experience at a payments company, bank, or fintech
- Familiarity with A/B testing platforms and experimentation culture
- Exposure to Amplitude, Mixpanel, or similar product analytics tools

Nice to have
- Technical background (CS degree or engineering experience) — a plus, not
  required
- Certification in product management (CSPO, Pragmatic Institute, etc.) is a
  bonus but not evaluated heavily

What success looks like
- Fraud loss rate trending down quarter over quarter without materially
  increasing false positives
- Onboarding friction stays low even as risk controls tighten
- Cross-functional stakeholders (compliance, data science, engineering) see
  you as the person who translates ambiguity into a shippable plan

About you
You thrive in ambiguity and don't need a fully-formed spec to start moving.
You're comfortable being the most senior person in the room on some days and
the most junior on others — this is a flat team and you'll wear a lot of
hats in the first six months. We're a fast-growing company, so this role
will evolve; the person who takes it should be excited by that, not
unsettled by it.

Compensation: competitive, based on experience. Location: Bengaluru,
hybrid (3 days/week in office).
